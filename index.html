Biography

Fermín López Marín, born May 11, 2003, in El Campillo, Spain, is a dynamic midfielder who represents the future of FC Barcelona. His journey from the youth academies to the first team showcases dedication, talent, and the classic Barcelona DNA.

Known for his versatility, technical ability, and vision on the field, López has quickly established himself as one of the most promising young talents in Spanish football. His ability to play multiple positions and contribute both defensively and offensively makes him a valuable asset to any team.
